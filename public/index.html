About This Tool

This application uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze Telegram channels and determine if they might be fake or spreading misinformation, especially in scientific contexts.

Key Features:
  • Analyzes channel content for signs of impersonation
  • Identifies suspicious patterns in scientific communication
  • Detects inconsistencies with established scientific knowledge
  • Provides probability scores for channel authenticity
  • Offers detailed reasoning for conclusions
